While I have several years of experience using Solidworks, Creo and Inventor, I prefer to use the latest cloud-based CAD software - Onshape, for the following reasons:

  • Faster feedback: Open the live model in your browser and comment directly.
  • Shareable space: All project artifacts are stored securely in the cloud, so we can access from anywhere.
  • Everything together: Model, BOMs and datasheets in one version-controlled space.
  • Branched concepts: We can explore different concepts in parallel, and choose the best.
  • Secure & resilient: Enterprise-grade security and continuous backups, so your data is always safe.
  • Full history: Every change is tracked and reversible.
