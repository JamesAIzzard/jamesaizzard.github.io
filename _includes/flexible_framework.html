Flexible Project Framework

Every project follows its own path. Instead of a rigid, one-size-fits-all process, I use a flexible framework of stages that can be added, removed, or reordered to fit the job. Small projects may only need one or two steps; larger ones may use the full set. By building and testing early, we cut risk quickly and cheaply, making it easier to adapt while changes are still simple and low-cost.
