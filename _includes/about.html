About me

I'm an experienced Chartered Mechanical Engineer. I hold a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering and a Master's degree in Intelligent Systems and Robotics, both 1st class. My design philosophy is built around three key ideas: doing less, but doing it better — removing unnecessary complexity to reveal what's essential; keeping solutions as simple as they can be, without losing what matters; and using rapid prototyping to learn quickly, adapt, and improve. My skills cover the whole development process, from early sketches and morphological analysis to generating fresh concepts. I'm proficient in 3D CAD (Onshape, SolidWorks, Creo, and Inventor) and technical drawing, and I use Python where needed to back designs with solid calculations. I'm experienced in a range of manufacturing processes, involving suppliers and stakeholders early, and creating clear, engaging artefacts that communicate design intent to potential investors.
