Manufacturing Experience

With over a decade in life-science product development, I've worked across a wide range of manufacturing processes - from the everyday to the highly specialised. If something unfamiliar comes up, I know how to track down the right expertise and get it made.

CNC Machining

CNC machining removes material from a stock block to produce the required geometry. Most prototypes and many products rely on machined parts, and I’ve designed components across a wide range of materials. Through close collaboration with local machining partners, I have rapid access to modern 5-axis CNC machines and CNC lathes. By reviewing designs directly with programmers, I ensure parts are optimised for their processes, with typical turnaround times of 5–10 working days in common materials.

Sheet Metal

I have extensive experience in sheet metal design and strong links with trusted local suppliers. Together we can approach tricky requirements and usually deliver parts within 7-10 working days. Finishes include welding, powder coating, anodising and more, in both low and high volumes.
